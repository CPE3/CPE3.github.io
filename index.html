Chris Peters - NMP Projects

Major Waterways of Illinois - MAP671, Module 3

This static map depicts the major waterways of Illinois using the Strahler stream order classification. Includes Lake Michigan.

2018 County Population Density- MAP671, Module 4

This map displays county population density using 2018 census data. Includes Hawaii and Alaska in addition to the lower 48 states.

Hawaiian Lava Flow - MAP671, Module 10

This is a static map showing the lava flow path following the 2015 eruption of Kiloeau.

Adventures in Flatland - MAP672, Module 3

This simple map displays the populations and latitude/longitude coordinates of some of the Midwestern cities I've often visited or lived in.

US Power Plants, Total and Renewable Capacity - MAP672, Module 7

This map depicts hydropower, wind energy, and solar energy production throughout the United States. All generating power plant points were obtained from the U.S. Energy Information Administration's (eia's) Form EIA-923.

Denver Amenities - MAP672, Module 8

Find fire stations, food, and after school programs within a kilometer of any Denver location!
